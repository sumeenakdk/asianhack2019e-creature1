About US


Welcome to the society for stray animal welfare.

Introduction

The project is all about management of stray animal.This project is generally based in social-service.We believe that this earth is not an inherited property of human beings only but must be equally shared by all animals on the earth. Theory of evolution has proven that all modern animals including human beings have descended from a common ancestor, the primitive aquatic animal or unicellular organism. So they are our kin and kith, and do have equal right to share the earth indiscriminately. Further, they placed us in the highest position of all animals not only for their unilateral corruption but for their welfare and management.


Objective/Aim

  • 1. To protect and save stray animals.
  • 2. To utilize the stray animal in proper way.
  • 3. To give animal their rights.
  • 4. Make people aware about animals and their rights
